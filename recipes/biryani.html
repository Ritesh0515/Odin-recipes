Chicken biryani

Description

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic dish made with long-grain basmati rice, tender chicken,and a blend of spices.It's popular Indian recipe that combines meat and rice into a satisfying meal,often served on special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken (marinated)
  • 2 onions,sliced
  • 1/2 cup yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • Whole spices (bay leaf,cloves,cardomom,cinnamon)
  • Biryani masala
  • Coriander & mint leaves
  • Saffron milk
  • Oil/ghee, salt

Steps

  1. Soak rice for 30 mins and cook it until 70% done.
  2. Marinate chicken with yogurt,spices,and ginger-garlic paste for 1 hour.
  3. Fry onions until golden brown.
  4. Cook chicken until tender in spices.
  5. Layer cooked chicken and rice.Add fried onions,mint,coriander,and saffron milk.
  6. Seal the pot and dum cook (slow steam) for 20-25 mins.
  7. Serve hot with raita or salan.
