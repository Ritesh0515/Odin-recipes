Puran Poli

Description

Puran Poli is a traditional sweet flatbread from Maharashtra made during festivals like Holi , Guddi padwa and Ganesh Chaturthi. It is stuffed with a filling of chana dal(split Bengal gram) and jaggery, flavored with cardomom and nutmeg.

Soft on the outside with a sweet and rich interior, Puran Poli is best served with ghee or milk.It requires some patience but is totally worth the effort!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chana dal (split Bengal garam)
  • 1.5 to 1.75 cups water
  • 3/4 cup jaggery (or as required)
  • 1/4 cardomom poweder
  • 1.5 cups of whole wheat flour
  • 1-2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt as requred
  • Water as needed for dough

Steps

  1. Rinse and pressure cook the chana dal until soft but not mushy.
  2. Drain excess of water and mash the dal well.
  3. In a pan,mix mashed dal with jaggery and cook until the mixture thickens.
  4. Add cardomom and nutmeg(optional).Let the stuffing cool.
  5. Make dough with wheat flour,water,and a pinch of salt .Rest it for 30 mins.
  6. Take a portion of dough and flatten it,add stuffing and seal it.
  7. Roll out gently and cook on a tawa with ghee until golden on both sides.
  8. Serve warm with ghee or milk.
