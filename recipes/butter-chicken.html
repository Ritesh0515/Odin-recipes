Butter Chicken

Description

Butter Chicken,also known as Murgh Makhani, is a famous Indian curry made with tender chicken cooked in creamy,mildy spiced tomato - gravy. It's rich,flavorul,and pairs perfectly with naan or rice.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken
  • 1 cup yoghurt(for marination)
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder,tumeric,garam masala
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Salt,ginger-garlic paste,kasuri methi

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices.Rest for 1 hour.
  2. Cook marinated chicken in a pan or tandoor.Set aside
  3. Saute onions in butter,add tomato puree,and cook well.
  4. Add spices and cream.Simmer thet sauce.
  5. Add cooked chicken,kasuri methi,and simmer for 10 more mins.
  6. Serve hot with naan or rice.
